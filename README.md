 Call Center Performance Analysis Dashboard

 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer support call center data to understand customer behavior, representative performance, customer satisfaction, call volume trends, and operational efficiency.

The analysis was performed using Microsoft Excel Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Dashboard Visualizations to help management make data-driven decisions.



🎯 Business Objectives

The project aims to answer the following questions:

- How many calls are received from each customer?
- Who are the Top 10 customers based on call volume?
- Which customers contact the call center most frequently?
- How satisfied are our customers?
- Which customers have the highest and lowest satisfaction ratings?
- Is customer satisfaction consistent across all representatives?
- Which representative handles the highest number of calls?
- Which representative receives the highest customer satisfaction ratings?
- Which representative(s) may require customer service training?
- How busy is the call center during 2023?
- Which months receive the highest call volume?
- Which days of the week are busiest?
- Should additional staffing be considered during specific months?
- What is the average call duration?
- Which customers spend the longest time on calls?
- Is there any relationship between call duration and customer satisfaction?
- Identify customers who call frequently but contribute limited business value.
- Identify areas where operational efficiency can be improved.

🔍 Key Insights

1. A small group of customers generated the highest number of calls, indicating recurring support requirements.
2. Overall customer satisfaction remained positive with an average rating of approximately 3.89/5.
3. Representative workload was uneven, with some representatives handling significantly more calls than others.
4. March and April experienced the highest call volumes, making them peak operational periods.
5. Call duration showed little to no impact on customer satisfaction levels.



💡 Recommendations

1. Provide additional customer service training for representatives with lower satisfaction scores.
2. Increase staffing during peak months to efficiently manage higher call volumes.
3. Improve first-call resolution processes to reduce repeat customer calls and operational costs.



📈 Outcome

The dashboard enables management to:

- Monitor customer behavior.

- Identify peak workload periods.
- Improve operational efficiency.
